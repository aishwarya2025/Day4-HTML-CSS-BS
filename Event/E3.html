Grand Dandiya Night 2025



Date: October 12, 2025

Time: 9:00 AM onwards

Location: Celebration Grounds, Banglore

Description: Join us for a day of vibrant music, energetic dance, and festive celebrations at the Grand Dandiya Night 2025. Experience the joy of Navratri with traditional Garba and Dandiya Raas. Delicious food and exciting prizes await!
